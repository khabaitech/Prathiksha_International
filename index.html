Prathiksha International is a world-wide merchandise-sourcing organization, specialising in manufacture and export of knitted readymade garments to the international market, located in Tirupur, the knitwear capital of India. We supply retail fashion outlets, boutiques and private label companies with affordable designer apparel, manufactured to demanding specification.

Our highly skilled staff and good network of suppliers have allowed us to consistently, deliver high quality garments since its inception in 2008. This has earned us a reputable name in the market as well as retaining many long term clients from all over the world. It is our interest to help our clients to succeed by delivering good value and high quality products.
